DIYA Website

The aim of the website is to provide a standard source for communication and manage resources for DIYA organization. The website will include links/drives of variety of spiritual material/notes etc. It will also provide common template resources to help accelerate Bal Sankar shala. It will also host general camp planning resources to help standarize the camp experience and ensure quality. To standardize communications, the site hosts a list of open spirtual study sessions, YOGA class session, and camp planning sessions, which users can request to join through forms. In addition, members will be able to sign up for volunteer events and/or have the ability to plan events (after they are vetted). The goal is to provide a common platform for members to learn and connect with resources/people to help with their spiritual journey.
